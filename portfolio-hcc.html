Cancer Survival Prediction

Project information

  • Category: Logistic Regression
  • Client: Study
  • Project date: 30 May, 2021
  • Project URL: https://github.com/natmurad/cancersurvivalprediction

The dataset used here was collected at a University Hospital in Portugal. It contains real clinical data of 165 patients diagnosed with HCC. It was downloaded from Kaggle. The goal is to predict if a person will survive or not one year after the diagnosis. It contains several information like symptons, alcohol, hepatitis, metastasis, gender, cirrhosis and etc. The main challenges on this dataset are the unballanced classes, high number of missing data and small sample.
